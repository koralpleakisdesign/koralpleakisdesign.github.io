About Koral

Design is everything
people interact with.

Hi, I'm Koral Pleakis — named after Coral from Finding Nemo.

I've been a creative person for as long as I can remember. I fell in love with graphic design when I realized it was behind everything people interact with — the ads I loved as a kid, the packaging, the logos. I just never knew it had a name.

I hold a Bachelor of Design from PennWest Edinboro and two years of hands-on experience at Zippo Manufacturing, where I created custom artwork for everything from product launches to U.S. Marines commissions.

Currently available for freelance work and open to full-time opportunities.
